Introduction

It is our policy to provide our clients with the following risk notice regarding trading on prediction markets.

Trading on prediction markets carry a high degree of risk, and you should not trade in such markets unless you understand their nature, and your own exposure to risk, subject to your circumstances and financial situation. The possibility exists that you could sustain a loss of some or all of your initial deposit and therefore you should not place funds that you cannot afford to lose; that is, trade with amounts that, if lost, will not adversely impact your lifestyle and your ability to meet your financial obligations.

If you do not fully understand the risks associated with trading, you must seek independent advice from your financial advisor. All trading strategies are used at your own risk.

Although prediction markets can be used for risk management, they are not necessarily suitable for all trades.

Past performance is no guarantee of future performance, since future performance of underlying assets is unpredictable, and many external factors might influence the probabilities of the outcomes at any time. We recommend that you keep close tabs on your trades and their status, and monitor the fluctuation in prices, and any world events that might influence your trade prices.

Before trading, examine the market’s volume of trade, all positions, their marginal prices/probabilities, and all fees related to the market, and establish the risks and opportunity, and the charges that might incur.

Profits or losses from transactions denominated in a currency may be subject to fluctuations in exchanges.

The information contained in this web page does not constitute financial advice or a solicitation to buy or sell shares of any type. Therefore, only make trades in market topics in which you have profound knowledge. We will not accept liability for any loss or damage, including without limitation any loss of profit, which may arise directly or indirectly from use of or reliance on such information.

Markets of High Volatility

Prediction markets require trading against marginal prices of outcome tokens. Please note that the marginal prices of outcome tokens might be subject to slippage that may occur when underlying markets are volatile and marginal prices move rapidly, which might impact your position and the cost of your trade. Please note that we limit this slippage to a maximum of 5% beyond which the transaction gets rejected, in order to prevent higher volumes of loss.

Technology Related Risks

All funds during market trading are held in event contracts. Although event contracts are considered reliable and secure, no such system is guaranteed to be totally reliable at all times, as vulnerabilities might be discovered in the smart contracts, which might result in loss of funds, although this is unlikely.

There are risks associated with utilizing an Internet-based trading system including, but not limited to, the failure of hardware, software, and Internet connection. Since Gnosis does not control signal power, its reception or routing via Internet, configuration of your equipment or reliability of its connection, we cannot be responsible for communication failures, distortions or delays when trading via the Internet.

Risks associated with Oracles

In case of a market becoming invalid, if no oracle were able to resolve the market, user funds will be refunded, but fees will be non-refundable.

We are the only oracle for the Gnosis Ops markets. If we decided the wrong outcome, we will also refund customers out of our own funds.
